Our Story

PorkDry Co. was born in a small family kitchen in 1998. Our grandfather's recipe — passed down through three generations — uses only the finest free-range pork, hand-rubbed with natural spices, and dried low and slow over 72 hours.

No shortcuts. No chemicals. Just pure craft and passion for great-tasting, nutritious dry pork meat — sold by the kilo the way it was always meant to be.
